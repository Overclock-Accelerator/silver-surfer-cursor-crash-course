üß≥ Silver Surfer's Travel Tips

1

Always scout before you commit. Some planets look great from orbit but have terrible cosmic energy. Or Galactus wants to eat them.

2

Avoid pleasure planets if you're having an existential crisis. Trust me on this one. Looking at you, Contraxia.

3

Make friends with the locals. The Fantastic Four saved my cosmic soul. The Nova Corps gave me an honorary badge. Friends matter.

4

Dead planets make great meditation spots. No distractions, just you and the ruins of ancient civilizations pondering mortality.

5

If a planet is also a person, just go with it. The universe is weird. Accept it.

6

Keep a travel log. After centuries of wandering, it all blurs together. Document the important stuff.
